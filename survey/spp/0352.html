Stem borer (Diptera: Agromyzidae) in Osmorhiza

Record Details

[?] Top
Record no.:0352
Feeding guild:Stem borer
Taxonomy:Diptera: Agromyzidae: Melanagromyza sp.
Stages observed:trace, puparium, adult
Distribution observed:IA
Hosts in Osmorhiza:undetermined O. sp., O. claytonii or O. longistylis (sweet cicely, aniseroot)

The puparium of this borer is formed in the stem; adults emerge in spring. I identified the host to genus only; Osmorhiza claytonii and O. longistylis are the two species in the host genus in the geographic area covered by the survey.

I reared an adult female in 2022 and an adult male in 2024. Both possessed a blue-green iridescence on the abdomen. The 2024 individual shared its stem with a Listronotus weevil (record 0350).
