Internal feeder (Diptera: Chloropidae) in Andropogon

Page contents:
  • Record Details●
  • References

Record Details

[?] Top
Record no.:0052
Feeding guild:Internal feeder in stem
Taxonomy:Diptera: Chloropidae
Stages observed:trace, puparium, adult
Distribution observed:IA
Hosts in Andropogon:A. gerardii (big bluestem)

I found puparia in shoots with killed tips, which I had originally collected because I determined while in the field that one contained a Lepidoptera larva tunneling inside (record 0054). Some shoots also contained cecidomyiid larvae (record 0051). Puparia of the chloropid were present by mid-August, with adults emerging in late August. I did not observe or record details about this insect's feeding behavior, and chloropids as a family exhibit a diverse array of feeding strategies including phytophagy but also saprophagy (Sabrosky 1987), so this record is listed tentatively.
