Record Details

[?] Top
Record no.:0328
Feeding guild:Stem miner
Taxonomy:Lepidoptera: Gracillariidae: cf. Marmara sp.
Stages observed:trace, larva
Distribution observed:IA
Hosts in Menispermum:M. canadense (moonseed)

A stem mine with a live and active larva and a second mine with a dead larva were found at the end of July, and evacuated stem mines were located in October. The mines at least sometimes contain a central frass line. The larva appears to belong to the genus Marmara (Gracillariidae).
