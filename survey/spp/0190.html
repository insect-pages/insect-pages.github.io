Record no.:0190
Feeding guild:Stem borer
Taxonomy:Diptera: Agromyzidae: Melanagromyza sp.
Stages observed:trace, puparium
Hosts in Daucus:D. carota (Queen Anne's lace, wild carrot)

Puparia were found overwintering in upper stems (above the lowest branching point of the stem). The general appearance of the puparium is consistent with Melanagromyza.
