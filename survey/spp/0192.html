Stem borer (Diptera: Agromyzidae) in Desmodium

Record Details

[?] Top
Record no.:0192, 0194
Feeding guild:Stem borer
Taxonomy:Diptera: Agromyzidae: cf. Melanagromyza sp.
Stages observed:trace, larva, puparium, adult
Distribution observed:IA
Hosts in Desmodium:D. illinoense (Illinois tick-trefoil); undetermined prairie D. sp. (tick-trefoil)

Tunnels in stem of host; puparium formed internally. The morphology of the posterior spiracles of final-instar larva and puparium -- with their slender, almost needle-like horns and reddish spiracular plates positioned so close to each other that they may appear partly fused -- is unusual among internally pupating stem borer agromyzids I observed in the survey.
