Stem borer (Coleoptera: Mordellidae) in Smilax

Page contents:
  • Record Details●
  • Featured Images

Record Details

[?] Top
Record no.:0509, 0658
Feeding guild:Stem borer
Taxonomy:Coleoptera: Mordellidae
Stages observed:trace, larva, adult
Distribution observed:IA
Hosts in Smilax:S. ecirrhata (upright carrion flower); undetermined S. sp., long herbaceous vine, apparently either S. illinoensis (Illinois carrion flower) or S. lasioneura (Blue Ridge carrion flower)

I located larvae overwintering in their frass-filled tunnels in herbaceous Smilax stems in January 2022 and February 2024, and reared an adult from S. ecirrhata in 2024. There were two whitish larvae in the same stem in the 2024 example. The adult was reddish overall.
