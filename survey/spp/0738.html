Stem miner (Diptera: Agromyzidae) in Barbarea

Page contents:
  • Record Details

Record Details

[?] Top
Record no.:0738
Feeding guild:Stem miner
Taxonomy:Diptera: Agromyzidae: Ophiomyia
Stages observed:trace, puparium
Distribution observed:IA
Hosts in Barbarea:B. vulgaris (yellow rocket)

I observed Ophiomyia stem mines and puparia on a cruciferous plant tentatively identified as B. vulgaris in early August, 2024. At least 2 puparia, both spent, were present under the epidermis at the ends of their stem mines. Photos of the host plant are available at https://www.inaturalist.org/observations/336308547.
