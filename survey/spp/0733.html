Record Details

Record no.:0733
Feeding guild:Stem miner
Taxonomy:Lepidoptera: Gracillariidae: Marmara sp.
Stages observed:trace
Distribution observed:IA
Hosts in Laportea:L. canadensis (wood nettle)

I came across a tentatively identified Marmara mine on a stem of wood nettle in mid-July, 2025. The long, winding linear mine was brown or reddish-brown in color with a central line of dark frass. I did not observe the larva directly.
