Internal feeder (Coleoptera: Curculionidae) in Capsella

Page contents:
  • Record Details

Record Details

[?] Top
Record no.:0123
Feeding guild:Miner/borer in petiole and stem
Taxonomy:Coleoptera: Curculionidae: cf. Ceutorhynchus sp.
Stages observed:trace, larva, adult
Distribution observed:IA
Hosts in Capsella:C. bursa-pastoris (shepherd's purse)

Most larvae I observed were tunneling in the bases of leaf petioles, and one was constructing a shallow, externally visible tunnel in a stem. The frass was bright green. Pupation of reared individuals occurred in the moistened potting mix in the bottom of the rearing container. Several adults emerged in late May from larvae collected in early May.
