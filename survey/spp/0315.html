Stem borer (Coleoptera: Erotylidae) in Lobelia

Page contents:

Record Details

[?] Top
Record no.:0315
Feeding guild:Stem borer
Taxonomy:Coleoptera: Erotylidae: Languria angustana
Stages observed:trace, pupa, adult
Distribution observed:IA
Hosts in Lobelia:L. siphilitica (great blue lobelia)

I found a pupa in a tunnel in a stem of L. siphilitica in early September, 2021. The adult emerged by late September, and it was identified to species by T. Hedlund on BugGuide.net (BG# 2113382).
