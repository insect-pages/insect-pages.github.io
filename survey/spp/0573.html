Stem miner (Lepidoptera: Gracillariidae) in Thalictrum

Page contents:
  • Record Details●
  • Featured Images

Record Details

[?] Top
Record no.:0573
Feeding guild:Stem miner
Taxonomy:Lepidoptera: Gracillariidae: cf. Marmara
Stages observed:trace, larva
Hosts in Thalictrum:T. dioicum (early meadow rue)

I found a young larva mining a stem of the host in early July. The linear mines were long, narrow, and winding with a brown discoloration and a central line of black frass.
