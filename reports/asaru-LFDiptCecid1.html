Local feeder (Diptera: Cecidomyiidae) in rhizomes of Asarum [0083]

OrderDiptera
FamilyCecidomyiidae[L]
Lower taxonNeolasioptera sp.
No. spp. involvedOne confirmed [1-C]
Feeding modeLocal feeder in rhizome
Host plantWild ginger, Asarum canadense (Aristolochiaceae)

Several larvae of this cecidomyiid were found in blackened chambers inside rhizomes of the host. There was no immediately obvious external sign of the larvae's presence. Specimens were shared with Ray Gagné, who determined them to be a species of Neolasioptera, a first for this host.
