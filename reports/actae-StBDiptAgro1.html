Stem borer (Diptera: Agromyzidae) in Actaea [0012]

OrderDiptera
FamilyAgromyzidae[T,P,A]
Lower taxoncf. Melanagromyza
No. spp. involvedOne confirmed
Feeding modeStem borer
Host plantBaneberry, Actaea sp. (Ranunculaceae)

Tunnels and pupariates in the lower stem. Puparium overwinters. An adult was reared in 2022.
